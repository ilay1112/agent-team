# Ops Board (business operations, finance, workforce)

Home of `BIZ` and `HR` ticket areas: cost reviews, vendor/account ops, downgrades/kills, agent
upgrades and capability reviews. Participants: coo, cfo, hr; orchestrator for triage. **Protocol,
entry block, and entry types are exactly `TEAM_BOARD.md`'s** — read selectively, append-only,
≤80 words, pointers over prose.

Why a separate board: monthly finance/HR loops produce threads no developer needs to scan; keeping
them here keeps the delivery board's hot reads cheap. Escalations that need the human owner queue
for the orchestrator's owner pulse (LIFECYCLE.md) unless a cap breach forces immediate escalation.

Archive: COO's weekly sweep moves Done threads to `boards/OPS_BOARD_ARCHIVE.md`; keep the hot board
<300 lines.
